Orthopedics and Spine Centers of Puerto Rico

Heal, Improve, Thrive: Orthopedic and Spine Care at Its Best

At Orthopedic Spine Centers of Puerto Rico, we are committed to providing you with exceptional care in a compassionate and friendly atmosphere. Located in the heart of our community, we believe that our patients deserve the best medical care, and we make an effort to ensure you always feel welcome and at ease. Whether you're coming in for a simple checkup or a more complex treatment regimen, our dedicated team of specialists has the skills and resources to manage all your orthopedic and spine health needs. Join us as we work together to improve your wellness and mobility.
